The **NIST Cybersecurity Framework (CSF)** is a widely used, voluntary framework that helps organizations **manage and reduce cybersecurity risk**. It was created by **National Institute of Standards and Technology (NIST)** and works for orgs of _any size or sector_.



1. **<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">Identify</mark>** – Know your assets, risks, and dependencies  
    _Asset management, risk assessment, governance_

2. **<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">Protect</mark>** – Put safeguards in place  
    _Access control, training, data security_

3. **<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">Detect</mark>** – Spot incidents fast  
    _Continuous monitoring, anomaly detection_

4. **<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">Respond</mark>** – Act when incidents happen  
    _Incident response, communications, mitigation_

5. **<mark style="background: #D2B3FFA6;">Recover</mark>** – Restore and improve  
    _Recovery planning, lessons learned_
