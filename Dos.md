![[difference-between-dos-vs-ddos.webp]]

<mark style="background: #ABF7F7A6;">defenition</mark>

In simple terms:  
It’s when someone **intentionally overloads a website, server, or network** so real users **can’t access it**.

An attacker floods a system with:

- <mark style="background: #FFB8EBA6;">too many requests</mark>,
- too much <mark style="background: #FFB8EBA6;">traffic</mark>, or
- <mark style="background: #FFB8EBA6;">resource-heavy actions</mark>

The system gets overwhelmed and slows down, crashes, or becomes unavailable.
