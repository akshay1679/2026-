## What information does HIPAA protect?

HIPAA protects **PHI (Protected Health Information)**, which includes:

- Patient names, addresses, phone numbers
- Medical records and diagnoses
- Test results and lab reports
- Insurance details
- Billing information
- Any health data linked to an identifiable person
